FAQs of CENTER PIVOT WINDOW:

FAQ

Q: 1 What is a Center Pivot Window?

A:A Center Pivot Window is a type of window that rotates around a central vertical axis. This allows the window to open inward or outward from the center, providing excellent ventilation and easy cleaning access.

Q: 2 Can Center Pivot Windows be customized?

A: Absolutely! Center Pivot Windows can be customized in terms of size, frame color, glass type, and hardware to fit your specific design needs.

Q: 3 Are there any design considerations for installing Center Pivot Windows?

A: Consider the placement and swing direction of the window to ensure it does not obstruct interior or exterior elements. Additionally, ensure there is sufficient clearance for the window to open fully.

Q: 4 What is the typical lead time for Center Pivot Windows?

A:Lead times can vary depending on customization options and manufacturing processes. On average, it may take 4-6 weeks from order to installation, but this can be confirmed with your supplier.
